ABOUT

I am a result oriented Full-Stack Web Developer with strong skill sets in both Front-End and Back-End. These skills have helped me to develop several full functional web applications.
Programming was always something that I considered that I could not do. I always thought I wasn't smart enough, logical enough, or even resilient enough to learn how to code. It was a very scary thought to leave the world of marketing, a world that I had more experience and knowledge in, for a world that seemed like the dark side of the moon at the time.
Fast forward, I knew I needed a career change in order to create a better future for myself. With that, coding was an adventure I wanted to explore. I took a few free online classes and then took the deep dive in to a structured program. Now here I am, ready to officially start my journey as a developer.
